# North Star

## Primary North Star

Users can open OpenButler once per day and understand within 60 seconds:

- what happened today,
- what is worth attention,
- what they should consider doing next.

## Supporting Metrics

- Today overview generation success rate.
- Number of insights with evidence boundaries.
- Ratio of insights accepted or marked useful by the user.
- Ratio of insights marked inaccurate.
- Average context recovery time.
- Count of measurable metrics covered by the daily overview.
- Ratio of capabilities available in strict mode.
- Local run success rate for new developers.

## Non-Goals for Metrics

- Do not optimize for notification count.
- Do not optimize for surveillance depth.
- Do not optimize for entertainment-site reduction unless the user has explicitly configured that goal.
- Do not treat generated text or screenshots as final facts for remote systems.
